Introduction

In the pilot study, you are going to collaborate with a humanoid robot on common household tasks. The robot will do most of the work, and you will help the robot by making choices using the provided web interface.

  • The task you will now go through is making orange juice.
  • During the process, there are several stages in which the robot needs your help. Each stage starts with a video clip. After watching the video, you will:
    1. ask the robot some questions (if available) and get an idea of what it is trying to do.
    2. answer some questions we prepared for you.
    3. finally, help the robot by indicating an action (based on your knowledge of the robot and the environment)
  • Be careful when you choose an action, as they have different effects (positive or negative) on the robot.

During this experiment please do not hit refresh button or "go back" button, otherwise the data we collected will become void
